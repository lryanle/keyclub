Our Mission Statement & Motto

The Moore High School Key Club, apart of the larger Texas-Oklahoma District, is a club centered around connecting our community through service and involvement. As a volunteering organization, we contribute to charitable causes and take part in organizations such as UNICEF, the Thirst Project, Children's Miracle Network Hospitals, and March of Dimes.
